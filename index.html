About Me

My Musical Foundation

Musical Background

I am a trained singer from Karnataka, with a deep-rooted foundation in Carnatic music since the age of 6. I am a disciple of Vid. MKN Acharya and hold a Junior certification.

Versatile Artist

My experience spans singing in various genres including Light music, Sugama Sangeetha, Folk songs and Film songs, allowing me to connect with diverse audiences.

Performance Experience

I have performed in Aaradhana programs and given stage performances across Bangalore, sharing my passion for classical music with communities.

Continuous Growth

Committed to refining my craft through rigorous training and exploring new dimensions of music to express my artistic vision authentically.

My Journey

From Passion to Profession

My passion for music took root at a very young age in Bangalore. As I started learning classical music, I was parallely performing aaradhanas held by my music guru and inter-school competitions in Sugama Sangeetha genre. From there my passion for music excelled and I started performing in inter-college competitions by forming a band called 'Indian Music team'.
